Our Story

Zhiping Shi Dance studio is a professional ballet training institution located in Milpitas, California. Since opening, we have been under the direction of studio owner and head teacher Zhiping Shi. Our unique curriculum is influenced by the Russian style of ballet and traditional Chinese dance.

Our Students

Zhiping Shi Dance studio offers graded classes from pre-ballet to pre-professional training to match each student's interests. We are also known for our point classes and traditional Chinese dance classes. After accepting a new student, we place the student in classes based on age and level of experience. For more information, visit our Curriculum page or contact us.
