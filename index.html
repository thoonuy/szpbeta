Our Story

Zhiping Shi Dance is a professional ballet training institution located in Milpitas, California. Since opening, we have been under the direction of studio owner and head ballet teacher Zhiping Shi. Our unique curriculum is influenced by the Russian style of ballet and traditional Chinese dance.

Our Students

We offer a major theater performance every year. Each student gets an opportunity to showcase their talents and what they have accomplished in the past year, some students are also invited to perform and participate in various community events.
