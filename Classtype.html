CLASS TYPES

We offer a range of dance classes in various different styles, including ballet, pre-ballet, Chinese dance, and point. Our classes cater to students of all ages and skill levels, and each class is taught by an expert dance instructor. See more about pricing information or free trial class

ADULT

This is an advanced ballet class en pointe for students who have demonstrated mastery of beginner and intermediate ballet skills. Adult students will learn techniques of professional ballet en pointe. Students in this class are 18 years and older.

CHINESE

This is a multi-level series of classes. Students will learn the skills of Chinese folk dance, as well as dance fundamentals and flexibility. This class is open to students of all ages, and students in the first level class are typically 6 years old.
